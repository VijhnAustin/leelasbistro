About

The Leela Vernon Bistro was founded in honor of the late Leela Vernon in October of 2018 by the proprietor Mr. Franz Vernon and his sister Mrs. Julet Austin-Vernon.

Mrs Lila Vernon's achievements include being declared a member of the British Empire (MBE) and a national hero of Belize for being a lifetime progenitor of kriol culture, cuisine, art, and tradition.

The Chef? Mr. Franz Kevin Vernon Sr. himself, the son of Miss Leela Vernon

We are proud of our cuisine and culture.
